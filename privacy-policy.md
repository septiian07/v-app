# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 5/22/2026  
**App Name:** V-App  
**Developer:** Septian Yoga  
**Contact:** s.yogapermadi@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how V-App collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

**Information We Collect**

We collect only the information necessary to operate the app and provide its features.

- Riot Games authentication information when you sign in through Riot OAuth.
- Account-related data returned by Riot services, such as your user ID and profile data.
- Authentication tokens and session cookies needed to keep you signed in and restore sessions.
- Region, locale, and client version values used to support app functionality and API requests.
- Crash and diagnostic data collected automatically through Firebase Crashlytics, such as app crashes, stack traces, device type, operating system version, and app version.

**How We Use Information**

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate you through Riot Games.
- Keep your session active and restore login state.
- Display account, profile, and Valorant-related data.
- Make authorized requests to Riot and related game services.
- Improve app stability and diagnose crashes or errors.
- Support language, region, and client-specific behavior.

**Local Storage**

The app stores some data locally on your device using secure storage, including:

- Access tokens
- Refresh tokens
- Entitlements tokens
- User ID
- Riot session cookies
- Region and locale settings
- Client version information

This data stays on your device unless you log out, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

**Third-Party Services**

The app uses third-party services to operate its features:

- Riot Games OAuth and related APIs for authentication and account access.
- Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics.
- Valorant and Riot-related API services for game data and account features.

These services may process your information according to their own privacy policies.

**Sharing of Information**

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only when necessary to:

- Authenticate your account with Riot Games.
- Send authorized requests to Riot or Valorant-related services.
- Collect crash reports and diagnostics through Firebase Crashlytics.
- Comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

**Data Retention**

We keep locally stored authentication data only as long as needed for app functionality or until you log out, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

Crash reports and diagnostics collected by Firebase Crashlytics are retained according to Google/Firebase policies.

**Data Security**

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. Sensitive authentication data is stored using secure device storage where supported. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

**Your Choices**

You can:

- Log out to remove locally stored authentication data.
- Clear app data or uninstall the app to delete local information on your device.
- Stop using the app if you do not agree with this policy.

**Children’s Privacy**

The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

**International Data Transfers**

Because the app uses third-party services such as Riot Games and Firebase, your information may be processed in countries outside your own.

**Changes to This Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date and publish the revised version in the app or on the app listing.
